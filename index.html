About us

Mountain Stream Organics was founded in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Asheville, NC, driven by a mission to provide all-natural, plant-based botanical products that can improve and enhance your quality of life. Our flagship product is premium, organic Kratom (Mitragyna speciosa). We globally source from only the finest Kratom plantations in Southeast Asia, and have a rotating inventory of the best strains based on the seasonal harvest. Our partner farms all hand-prune their Kratom trees in an ecologically friendly manner, with only the most mature leaves being selected for harvest.

Mountain Stream Organics is a trusted online and wholesale supplier that delivers superior products at competitive prices while providing the best customer experience in the industry.
